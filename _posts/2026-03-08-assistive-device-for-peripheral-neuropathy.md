title: Assistive Device for Peripheral Neuropathy
---

For senior deisgn, I worked with a group of friends to develop a device for pateients with peripheral neuropathy. One way peripheral neuropathy manifests is in numbness and lack of sensation in the feet. As such, it can greatly limit the mobility of patients. This device provided a way to gain that independence back by providing the feedback of pressure at the front, side, and rear of the foot to vibrotactile feedback on the leg. Our device used bluetooth connectivity so it could wirelessly connect between a shoe insert and the leg band and so the leg band could be moved on the leg wherever the weater wished for comfort and fashion as well as if the nubmess continued to degenerate up from the feet. 

This device won first place of 68 teams at the [2023 Malott Innovation Awards](https://engineering.purdue.edu/ME/News/2023/winning-senior-design-project-helps-people-with-neuropathy-learn-to-walk-again).
