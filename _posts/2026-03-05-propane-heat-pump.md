After many people's initial summer plans fell through for 2020, including mine, the ME department at Purdue did a program of remote summer research projects. I chose to work on a two stage heat pump using R-290 (Propane) as the refrigerant for use in cold climates. With this project I designed a preliminary field prototype system in Solidworks. This adapted the lab prototype to a functional unit that was able to be tested in the field after the summer project was completed.
