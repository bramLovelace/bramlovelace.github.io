title: Skateboard Brake
---

For my sophomore design project, I worked with a small team to create a hydraulically actuated brake that would engage when a skateboarder stepped of their board to stop runaway skateboards. This use a flexible chamber sandwiched between a truck and the board connected to a piston that would disengage a brake pad when pressure was applied by the weight of the rider.
