title: Pit Cart

To learn CAD and evaluate Onshape for my team when it was new in 2015, I designed an upgrade to our aging and heavy pit carts. These were designed to work as a pair, each holding 2 totes and 12 bins of components with storage for long material stock behind. We assembled them from 1/2" plywood and used them for the 2016 and 17 seasons without issue. 

We continued to use Onshape in a small capacity for the team until 2019 when the team formally moved from Solidworks to Onshape.
